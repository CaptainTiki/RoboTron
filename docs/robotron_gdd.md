# HYPE ARENA – Game Design Document

## Core Fantasy
You are a gladiator in a televised death sport where survival isn’t enough—you must entertain the crowd to earn money and climb the ranks.

## Core Gameplay Loop
1. Enter Arena
2. Fight enemies
3. Build HYPE through risky actions
4. Earn money (multiplied by hype)
5. Survive or die
6. Spend earnings / bid into next show

## Core Pillars
- Risk = Reward
- Performance Over Survival
- System-Driven Chaos
- Diegetic Presentation

## Core Systems

### Combat
- First-person arena shooter
- 1–2 weapons (prototype)
- Basic enemy types

### Hype System
Generated by:
- Kills
- Mid-air kills
- Taking damage
- Low HP

Decays over time.

### Multiplier
- 0–100 = x1
- 100–200 = x2
- 200+ = x3+

### Economy
- Money earned per kill × multiplier

### Sponsors
- Pre-match contracts
- Mid-match challenges

### Jumbotron
- Displays hype, multiplier, kills
- Reacts to gameplay

### Announcer
- Reacts to gameplay signals

### Arenas
- Introduce hazards and verticality

### Progression
- Spend money to enter higher-tier arenas
