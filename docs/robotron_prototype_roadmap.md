# HYPE ARENA – Prototype Roadmap

## Prototype Goal
Is it fun to play recklessly for money?

## MUST HAVE

### Player
- Movement
- Jump
- Shooting

### Enemy
- Basic AI (chase + attack)

### Arena
- Flat + one platform
- One hazard

### Hype System
- Gain from kills, damage, low HP
- Decay over time

### Multiplier
- x1 → x3 scaling

### Money
- Earn per kill × multiplier

### Sponsors
- 2 pre-match
- 1 mid-match

### Jumbotron
- Displays hype, multiplier, kills

### Announcer
- Basic triggers

## Roadmap

### Session 1
Movement + shooting + enemy

### Session 2
Health + damage + arena

### Session 3
Hype system

### Session 4
Money + rewards

### Session 5
Sponsors

### Session 6
Jumbotron + announcer

## Success Criteria
- Risky play is fun
- Players chase hype
- Sponsors affect behavior
